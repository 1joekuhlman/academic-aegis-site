Defending Against Academic Allegations

With over 15 years of experience prosecuting serious violent felonies and defending capital murder cases, Joe Kuhlman has established an impressive litigation record, having conducted over 125 trials. Whether you are a student, employee, or faculty member, Joe's extensive qualifications provide practical guidance in addressing policy violations and significant Title IX issues.
